Catch the Phish

Decide if each message is PHISH or SAFE.

Scenario 1

From: security@paypaI-support.com

Subject: URGENT: Your account will be closed!

We detected unusual activity. If you do not confirm your information within 2 hours, your account will be closed. Click the link below:

https://paypaI-security.com/verify

Scenario 2

From: it-support@university.edu

Subject: Password Reset Confirmation

Your password was reset successfully. If this was NOT you, please call the IT Help Desk using the number on the official website.

Scenario 3

From: hr@company-jobs.com

Subject: You got the job! Fill out the attached form

To finish onboarding, fill out the attached form with your Social Security Number, bank details, and ID photo within 24 hours.

Tip: Watch for urgency, strange links, unknown senders, and requests for sensitive data.
